SUNGAI BULOH,2014: Learning Chance for Orphans; Rumah Aman Orphanage and Welfare Home will collaborate with Aidni Islamic International School to enable its young residents to get a good education. Its founder, businessman Abdul Rehman Dakri, said a good education with strong religious values was the foundation of success. Rehman said the objective of the collaboration was to ensure that every student of the home obtained a good education to prepare them for the job market once they leave school. The home houses 42 boys and the number is expected to increase to 70 by next year.
